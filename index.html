Dan Heng: 4-star

Role

- Single-target Wind DPS (The Hunt) - Best for boss fights and elite enemies

Key Mechanics

- Skill → On CRIT, 100% base chance to Slow enemy (SPD -12% for 2 turns) - Ultimate → Deals bonus damage if the enemy is Slowed - Talent → When an ally targets Dan Heng, his next attack gains 36% Wind RES PEN (2-turn cooldown) > Golden Rule: Always use Ultimate after an ally buffs Dan Heng and his Skill has Slowed the enemy.

Best Light Cones

1. In the Night (5★, Seele’s sig) – BIS 2. Cruising in the Stellar Sea (5★, Herta Shop) – Best F2P 3. Swordplay (4★) / Only Silence Remains(4★)

Best Relics

Set:4pc Eagle of Twilight Line (Wind DMG + action advance on Ultimate) Ornaments: 2pc Rutilant Arena (needs 70% CRIT Rate) Main Stats: - Body: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG - Feet: SPD - Sphere: Wind DMG - Rope: ATK% Sub-stats: CRIT Rate → CRIT DMG → ATK% → SPD

Best Teams (Hypercarry)

- Buffer (triggers Talent): Tingyun (best 4★) / Bronya / Sparkle - Debuffer:Pela / Welt / Ruan Mei - Sustain: Huohuo / Fu Xuan / Gallagher / Natasha Beginner combo: Tingyun Skill on Dan Heng → Dan Heng Skill → Dan Heng Ultimate

Eidolons

- E1: +12% CRIT Rate vs enemies above 50% HP - E4: Ultimate advances action by 100% on kill - E6: Stronger Slow

Dan Heng: Imbibitor Lunae

Role

Imaginary Destruction DPS (Blast & High SP Consumption) – Best for 3-target scenarios and one-shot rotations

Key Mechanics

Enhanced Basic Attack (EBA) → Consumes 1/2/3 Skill Points (SP) for scaling DMG and hits (1/3/5 hits total) Skill (Squama Sacrosanct) → Makes next EBA not consume SP (once per 2 turns) and grants 1 stack of Outroar Talent → After EBA, deals 1 instance of Bonus DMG to all enemies hit Ultimate → Deals AoE Imaginary DMG and gains 2 Squama Sacrosanct stacks Golden Rule: Always aim for 3-SP EBA (full nuke). Use Ult to refresh Squama for a free 3-SP hit.

Best Light Cones

Brighter Than the Sun (5★, Sig) – BIS (energy, DMG, ATK) On the Fall of an Aeon (5★, Herta Shop) – Best F2P Something Irreplaceable (5★) / A Secret Vow (4★)

Best Relics

Set: 4pc Wastelander of Banditry Desert (Imaginary DMG + CRIT vs debuffed) OR 4pc Musketeer (ATK + SPD + Basic ATK DMG) Ornaments: 2pc Rutilant Arena (needs 70% CRIT Rate) – boosts Basic ATK DMG Main Stats: Body: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG Feet: ATK% or SPD (with fast supports) Sphere: Imaginary DMG Rope: ATK% or Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) for smoother rotations Sub-stats: CRIT Rate → CRIT DMG → ATK% → SPD

Best Teams (Hypercarry– SP Generators)

Core Supports: Sparkle (best – SP + DMG), Tingyun (energy + buffs), Yukong (Imaginary synergy) Debuffer: Pela / Silver Wolf Sustain: Luocha (SP-positive), Huohuo (energy), Fu Xuan (CRIT buff) Beginner combo: Tingyun Ult on DHIL → Sparkle Skill → DHIL 3-SP EBA → DHIL Ult → DHIL 3-SP EBA again

Eidolons

E1: EBA deals +40% more DMG to the first target on 2+ SP hits E2: After using Ult, action advances by 100% and gains 1 extra Squama stack – massive E6: Talent Bonus DMG triggers per each hit of EBA (instead of once)

Dan Heng: Permansor Terrae

Role

Phisical Preservation/Sub-DPS (Shield + Counter) – Best for sustaining while dealing off-turn damage

Key Mechanics

Skill → Grants a Shield to all allies (scales with DEF) for 3 turns. While shielded, allies gain 15% Effect RES. Talent → When a shielded ally is hit, Dan Heng performs a Counter (Imaginary DMG, scaling with DEF). Triggers once per ally per turn. Ultimate → Deals AoE Imaginary DMG and applies Root to all enemies (Imprisonment: Action Delay + SPD -10%) for 1 turn. Technique → Creates a defensive field for 15s. Entering battle gives all allies a Shield equal to 30% of Dan Heng's DEF + 200. Golden Rule: Keep Shields up on all allies to maximize Counter triggers. Use Ultimate before enemy turns to delay their actions.

Best Light Cones

Moment of Victory (5★, Gepard's sig) – BIS (DEF + Effect Hit Rate + Taunt) Texture of Memories (5★, Herta Shop) – Best F2P (DEF + RES) Landau's Choice (4★) / Day One of My New Life (4★)

Best Relics

Set: 4pc Knight of Purity Palace (DEF + Shield strength) OR 4pc Guard of Wuthering Snow (DMG reduction) Ornaments: 2pc Broken Keel (needs 30% Effect RES) – grants CDMG to team Main Stats: Body: DEF% / Effect Hit Rate (for Root) Feet: SPD / DEF% Sphere: DEF% / Imaginary DMG (for more Counter dmg) Rope: Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) OR DEF% Sub-stats: DEF% → SPD → Effect Hit Rate → Effect RES

Best Teams (Sustain + Counter Support)

Main DPS: Any squishy DPS (Seele, Jingliu, Blade) Sub-DPS/Synergy: Clara / Yunli (double counter comp) Buffer: Robin (teamwide ATK + action advance) / Ruan Mei (weakness break efficiency) Healer: Luocha / Huohuo (for emergency heals) Beginner combo: Dan Heng Skill (Shield) → Enemy hits shielded ally → Dan Heng Counter → Dan Heng Ultimate before enemy's next wave

Eidolons

E1: Counter now hits all enemies instead of single target E4: Shields last 1 additional turn. When a Shield expires, heals the ally for 15% of Dan Heng's DEF E6: Ultimate's Root cannot be resisted and lasts 2 turns. Counter DMG +100%
